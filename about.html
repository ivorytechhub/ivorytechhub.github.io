Our Story

Ivory Tech Hub was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality technology education and solutions accessible to everyone in Uganda.

What started as a small initiative to help fellow students with their final year projects has grown into a trusted technology partner for hundreds of students and businesses across the country.

We understand the challenges students face when approaching their final year projects, and we know the technology hurdles small businesses encounter. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to providing affordable, high-quality solutions that make a real difference.

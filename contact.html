Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does it take to complete a student project?

Most student projects are completed within 1-2 weeks, depending on complexity. We always discuss timelines upfront and deliver on schedule.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money in Ugandan Shillings (UGX). For business clients, we can also arrange bank transfers.

Do you provide support after project delivery?

Yes! All our projects come with free support (1-3 months depending on the package). We're always available to help with any issues.

Can I request changes to my project?

Absolutely! We include a certain number of revisions in each package. Additional changes can be accommodated at a reasonable cost.

Is the source code included?

Yes, all projects come with complete, well-documented source code. For free resources, you can download directly from GitHub.
